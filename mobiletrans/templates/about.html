About

This mobile app is designed for Chicago "L" users. Have you ever wondered what's around all the various CTA stops? Have you ever wondered which stop is closest to your current location? Wonder no longer. Windy Transit helps Chicago travelers find locations of interest around the CTA stop of their choice. This web-service backed android application makes finding this information a breeze. The search returns Hospitals, Libraries, Landmarks, and Bus Stops.

Using a sophistocated GEO-spacial web service api, this app queries a datawarehouse populated with geographic locations from all around Chicago. As this backend is higly customizable, more types of data will soon be added.
